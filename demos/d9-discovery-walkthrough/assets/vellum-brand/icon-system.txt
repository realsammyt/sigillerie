Pick rationale (Phase 4, iconography slot):
Hara-Whitespace direction defaults to Lucide thin or Phosphor light.
Picked Lucide thin (1.25 stroke) over Phosphor light because the existing
type-stack already runs on a thin-weight register (Inter Light wordmark,
Source Serif 500 display), and stroke-weight harmony beats glyph-count.

Use rules:
- 1.25 stroke weight (the default thin Lucide variant)
- 18px and 24px sizes only, no 16px (too crowded with our tracking)
- icons never carry color, they take currentColor from --ink or --accent-quiet
- never substitute a custom icon mid-flow; if Lucide doesn't have it,
  re-write the affordance to use one Lucide already ships
- pair every icon with a text label until usability research says otherwise
